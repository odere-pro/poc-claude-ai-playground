EMPLOYMENT CONTRACT — ANONYMIZED FIXTURE (SE)

Mixes 1 illegal, 2 compliant, and 1 unchecked clause.
Used by the SE jurisdiction-swap e2e test.

----------------------------------------------------------------

§ 1 — Provanställning
The employee shall serve a probation period of nine (9) months,
during which either party may terminate without notice.

§ 2 — Arbetstid
Regular working time is 40 hours per week, Monday through Friday.
Overtime is compensated according to collective agreement terms.

§ 3 — Uppsägningstid
Notice of termination by either party is one (1) month for the
first two years of service, increasing per LAS thereafter.

§ 4 — Sekretess
The employee agrees to keep proprietary information confidential
both during and after employment.
