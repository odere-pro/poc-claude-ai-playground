SAMPLE EMPLOYMENT CONTRACT — FOR TESTING ONLY (CLEAN STATE)

Between: Acme Logistics B.V. ("Employer")
And:    [Worker Name] ("Employee")
Date:   1 March 2026

The Employer and the Employee agree as follows.

1. NON-COMPETE
   No non-compete clause applies. The Employee is free to take up
   any role with any employer after termination.

2. PROBATION PERIOD
   The Employee is engaged on an indefinite-term contract with a
   probation period of 1 month from the start date, agreed in writing.

3. NOTICE PERIOD
   The Employer shall provide 2 months written notice; the Employee
   shall provide 1 month written notice. Notice takes effect at the
   end of the calendar month.

4. OVERTIME
   Overtime, when required, is compensated at 125% of the base hourly
   rate and is capped at 8 additional hours per week, well within
   Arbeidstijdenwet limits averaged over the reference period.

5. WORKING HOURS
   Standard working time is 40 hours per week, Monday to Friday.
   The Employee is entitled to 11 consecutive hours of daily rest
   and 36 consecutive hours of weekly rest.

6. WORK-FROM-HOME POLICY
   The Employer provides a laptop and ergonomic equipment.
   Utilities are reimbursed at €40 per month.

Signed at Amsterdam.
