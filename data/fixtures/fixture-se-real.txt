Between: Nordic Logistics AB ("Employer")
And:    [Worker Name] ("Employee")
Date:   1 March 2026

The Employer and the Employee agree as follows.

1. PROBATION
   The Employee is engaged on a probationary basis for a period
   of 7 months. The role converts to permanent employment if the
   Employer does not give notice during the probation.

2. NOTICE PERIOD
   The Employee, who has 5 years of continuous service in similar
   roles within the group, is entitled to 3 months written notice
   from the Employer. The Employee shall give 1 month written notice.

3. WORKING HOURS
   Standard ordinary working time is 40 hours per week, with 11
   consecutive hours of daily rest and 36 consecutive hours of
   weekly rest.

4. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
   All work output created by the Employee in the course of
   employment is assigned to the Employer.

Signed at Stockholm.
