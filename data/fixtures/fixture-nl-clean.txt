EMPLOYMENT CONTRACT — ANONYMIZED FIXTURE (NL CLEAN)

This fixture is fully compliant with NL labor law. Used by the
clean-path e2e test to verify the "looks compliant" UI state.

----------------------------------------------------------------

Article 1 — Trial period
The employee shall serve a probation period of one (1) month,
during which either party may terminate with at least one week's notice.

Article 2 — Working hours
Working hours are 36 hours per week. Average weekly hours over a
16-week period shall not exceed 48. Daily rest of at least 11
consecutive hours is guaranteed.

Article 3 — Notice period
Termination by either party requires at least one (1) month
written notice. After 5 years of service, employer notice
extends to 2 months.

Article 4 — Holiday allowance
The employee receives statutory holiday allowance of 8% of gross
annual salary, paid in May.

Article 5 — Confidentiality
The employee shall keep proprietary information confidential
during and for two years after employment.

Article 6 — Governing law
This contract is governed by Dutch law. Disputes shall be
resolved by the competent court in Amsterdam.
