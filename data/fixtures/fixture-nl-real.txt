EMPLOYMENT CONTRACT — ANONYMIZED FIXTURE (NL)

This fixture mixes 2 illegal, 2 compliant, and 2 unchecked clauses.
Used by the e2e happy-path test and as Phase-5 demo material.

----------------------------------------------------------------

Article 1 — Trial period
The employee shall serve a probation period of three (3) months,
during which either party may terminate without notice.

Article 2 — Working hours
Working hours are 40 hours per week, Monday through Friday, with
overtime compensated at 1.25x the regular rate.

Article 3 — Non-compete
For a period of two (2) years following termination, the employee
shall not, anywhere in the European Union, work in any industry
that the employer or its affiliates are engaged in.

Article 4 — Notice period
Either party may terminate this contract by providing one (1)
month written notice, in line with statutory notice periods.

Article 5 — Confidentiality
The employee agrees to keep all proprietary information confidential
both during and after employment, including but not limited to client
lists, source code, and strategic plans.

Article 6 — Dispute resolution
Any disputes arising from this contract shall be submitted to
binding arbitration in Amsterdam under Dutch law.
